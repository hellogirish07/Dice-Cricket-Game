Dice Cricket Game

Dice Cricket is a fun and simple game based on cricket, where two players (a batsman and a bowler) take turns rolling a dice to play the game. Here’s how it works:

Game Rules:

  • The game consists of two innings, one where the batsman plays and one where the bowler plays.
  • The player who bats rolls a dice, and the player who bowls rolls a dice as well.
  • If the bowler’s dice number is greater than the batsman’s number, the batsman is OUT.
  • If the batsman’s dice number is greater, the batsman’s score increases by the value of the dice roll.
  • Each player gets to roll the dice for 6 times (1 over), and after 6 rolls, the turn switches.
  • Once both players have had their turn to bat, the scores are compared, and the player with the highest score wins.

Steps to Play:

  1. Enter names for the batsman and bowler.
  2. Choose how many overs (1 to 5) you want to play.
  3. Click on "Start Game" to begin.
  4. Click on "Roll Dice" to play each turn.
  5. After both players finish their turn, the winner will be announced.
